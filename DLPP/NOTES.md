## Subject Notes

**Code:** 3174201 | **Credits:** 5 | **Total Marks:** 150
**Category:** Professional Core (mandatory)
**Syllabus:** [Deep-Learning-Principles-and-Practices-3174201.md](../syllabus/Deep-Learning-Principles-and-Practices-3174201.md)

## Unit Breakdown (48 hours total)

| Unit | Topic | Hours | Weightage |
|------|-------|-------|-----------|
| 1 | DL Basics, Applications, Challenges | 4 | 10% |
| 2 | Neural Networks (ANN, types, activation) | 10 | 25% |
| 3 | Neural Network Based Fuzzy Systems | 12 | 25% |
| 4 | TensorFlow, Transfer Learning, NLP Basics | 10 | 15% |
| 5 | DL Algorithms: CNN, LSTM, RNN, GAN, etc. | 12 | 25% |

## Practicals (Key ones)
1. NN model with one hidden layer for classification
2. MLP for prediction (e.g., house prices)
3. CNN for Image Classification
4. RNN/LSTM/GRU implementation
5. ANN on GPUs

## Exam Strategy
- Units 2, 3, 5 carry 75% of exam marks — prioritize these
- Unit 3 (Fuzzy Neural Systems) is unique/tricky — needs dedicated lessons
- Unit 5 has the most algorithms — make a cheat sheet reference
