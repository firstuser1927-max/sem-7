## Exam Strategy
- Pure theory — focus on definitions, processes, and legal aspects
- IT Act 2000, Certificate 65B, Locard's Principle = guaranteed exam questions
- Unit 5 (Network + Mobile Forensics) has most hours → most questions
- Unit 6 (Email Forensics) is very specific — email headers, types of attacks
- No practicals = more time for theory mastery
