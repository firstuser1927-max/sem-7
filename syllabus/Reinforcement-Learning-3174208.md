| WEF Academic Year : | 2023-24 |

| Category of the Course : | Professional Elective - VI |

Prerequisite: Linear algebra, probability, statistics, machine learning

Rationale : In this course, we will investigate the process of learning through an agent's mistakes. This is very dissimilar to supervised machine learning and more closely resembles how people learn via experience. The focus of Reinforcement Learning (RL) is on issues that call for a series of decisions to be made in order. The fundamentals of reinforcement learning are the focus of this course.
